My Favorite Ice Cream Parlors

Nathan's Dairy Bar

Nathan's Dairy Bar is a local ice cream shop in Manassas that was even the winner of the 2024 NoVA Ice Cream Wars

Jitterbug's

Jitterbug is a local ice cream shop in Old Town Manassas that gave ice cream to Counterspell Manassas. Their s'more flavor is to die for.

Yoder's Dutch Delights

Yoder's Dutch Delights originally started with donuts a few short weeks ago and has since evolved into donuts. Their ice cream donut sandwhich is impossibly good.
