Best Deserts

Ice Cream

Ice cream is nice because it has a sweet flavor and a smooth consistency. In larger servings, it can become a bit dull.

Funnel Cake

Funnel cake is a very delicious fried desert that comes in many different varieties and flavors. You can sit there for hours and hours

Bread Pudding

Bread pudding is an unholy creation made to disappoint all of man. It tastes extremely nasty and should be avoided at all cost.
